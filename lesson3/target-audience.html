Target Audience

The target audience for Weatherday is anyone in the local area who needs to check weather conditions. This can be someone who just wants to know what to to wear that day or someone in agriculture who needs to know what the weather will be for farming. The audience will likely be checking the weather on their smart phone while on the go.

Personas

Josh

Josh is a 38 year old husband and dad of three. He is an attorney by day and an outdoor enthusiast on the weekend. In his free time he enjoys camping, hiking, skiing, and mountain biking with his kids.

Elle

Elle is a 15 year old freshman who runs the weather report for her school's morning news program. She runs track, skis, and hikes on the weekends.


Scenarios

Scenario 01

Elle has gym today and needs to know if she has to run the mile or if it will be cancelled due to rain.

Scenario 02

Josh is taking his venturing crew camping Friday night. He needs to know how cold it will be so he knows what to pack.

Scenario 03

Josh needs to repair his roof. He needs to know if it will rain after work today.

Scenario 04

Elle has a pool party planned for her birthday. She needs to know what the high will be and if there is a chance of lightning.

Scenario 05

Josh is going mountain biking after work. He needs to know what time the sun is setting so he can get back home before dark.
