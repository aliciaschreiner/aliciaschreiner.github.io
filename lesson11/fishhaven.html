Cloudy Weather to Stay

By: Weatherday Editor


It may seem like we've been living in the Pacific Northwest with all of the cloud cover we've seen lately. I hope you'll get used to it and hopefully enjoy it, as we are seeing more clouds in Fish Haven's five day forecast.

Showers will also be on the way, so be sure to get your gardens and flower beds planted soon. Get out your umbrellas and rainboots and make the best of it! If you're out and about with your camera, be sure to snap some shots and send them into the Weatherday. We'll be sure to post them on the wesite. Stay safe out there!
