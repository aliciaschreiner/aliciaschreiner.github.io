Lesson 02: Design Principles

Alignment

Area 17

See Design

This is mobile screenshot of a design firm called Area 17. Their website is clean, organized, and uses Alignment in a beautiful way. All of the elements on the home page are left justified and the columns are nicely aligned. The page that shows their work uses a grid pattern and all of the images are perfectly aligned. They also make great use of white space and contrast.

Whitespace

Studio McGee

See Design

This is mobile screenshot of an interior design firm called Studio McGee. Their website has plenty of white space around their logo and the navigation bar. The entire wesite is organized well, uses grids, and leaves plenty of breathing room for the viewer's eye. It feels light, bright, and clean.

Hierarchy

Huncwot

See Design

This is mobile screenshot of a creative agency called Huncwot. On their website the logo is front and center and contrasts the background. The have a large image to the side with the opacity dropped down that your eye goes to next. Below that is an image in full opacity that shows their work in more detail. Then your eye finally makes it to the other side of the page with the details of their agency.
