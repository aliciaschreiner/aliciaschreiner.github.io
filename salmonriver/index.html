Adventure Awaits!

Expertly Guided Trips since 1998

Welcome to Salmon River Adventures where we take you on the trip of a lifetime! We offer half-day, full-day, and 3-day adventures down the beautiful Salmon River. Our river guides are highly experienced, thoroughly trained, and most of all love rafting the Salmon. Using our self-bailing rafts you will have a safe, fun, and unforgettable experience with Salmon River Adventures. We can't wait for you to join us!
