Full-Day Guided Trip

18 Miles

Pack a ton of whitewater into one day! Our exclusive 8-hour rafting trip is for you! Our Full Day trip is just what the doctor prescribes. We have a private dock and beach just for Salmon River Adventures. We'll feed you a delicious BBQ lunch on two acres of riverfront grass. We raft Heller Bar, Hammer Creek, and take some Class III rapids, so prepare to get wet!

3-Day Guided Trip

45 Miles

Get the most fun and excitement with our 3-Day - 45 mile trip! It's the complete adventure with lots of whitewater, delicious food, and camping under the stars. Our river guides will show you how great the Salmon is, rafting through Class III and IV rapids. Prepared for some adrenaline to pump through your veins!


Half-Day Guided Trip

10 miles

Our Half-Day trip is perfect for beginners or those with a tight schedule. The river run takes in the best whitewater stretches on the Salmon, including Deerhead, Wild Goose, and Eagle Creek. There is time to relax, swim, and view wildlife as well glide through Cougar and Geneva Bars. The perfect 4 hour trip!
